DIGITAL NOTICE BOARD APP REVIEW

About DNB

This is a digital notice board that updates users in real time on the go. The digital digital notice board is a mobile hand held device APP created for the Android mobile OS platform and the code base is in JAVA. Other mobile OS platform as well as a Native WEB APP will be added in future release. The back end of the APP where the notices will be uploaded is Googles most powerful spreed sheet (Google Sheet). This same spread sheet will be the initial database for the APP. The choice of using Google sheet as a database source and a back end updater of the APP is to makeit easy for everyone any where without any database experience to be able to update his audience without hustle. Another reason being that, notice can be updated on a normal excel sheet and push it to the Google sheet at any time. With this APP, complexity of IT tools are eliminated. It wont also cost more to train Administrators on how to push notice to their audience.The APP is designed for the general public. Anybody can apply for a digital notice board and within24 hours that person will have his Google Sheet account directly send to him/her. NOTE this plan for the future.Targeted initial audience of the APP are the students of the University of Ghana. Student who uses this APP will have real time access to their TIME TABLE and real time University update, all on the go. Student can request for any past TIME TABLE and download it to their respective device. Student will not be given the right to write to the notice board, ADMINISTRATION will have total permission to update the notice board (TIME TABLE part), however for any other updates apart for the TIME TABLE part SRC and ADMINISTRATION will also be given permission to update the main board.Updates on the notices board will automatically be remove within 24hr period if no new amendment has been made to it. TIME TABLES will also be remove with in the same 24hr period. Student will be notify when a new notice appear. Notification will be in the form of the device vibrating or ringing. To have access to the digital notice board you have to install the APP on your device and register with your USERNAME and PASSWORD.
